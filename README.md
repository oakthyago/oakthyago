## 👋 Hi, I'm Thyago

**Electrical Engineer** → **Developer** → **Data & AI**, working as a Business Specialist at Banco do Brasil.

My differentiator: I bring an **engineering mindset** to software. Years designing amd commissioning electrical systems  **AutoCAD** and **Revit**, modeling and simulating in **MATLAB/Simulink**, and a solid foundation in math, linear algebra and optimization — now applied to **Machine Learning, Data Science and AI**.

- 🔭 Currently studying for a **Data Scientist** public exam (TJ-RJ / CESGRANRIO) and FIAP postgrad Tech Challenges
- 🌱 Going deep on **software engineering**: clean code, testing, Git, project structure
- ⚡ From electrical systems to neural networks — same rigor, new domain
